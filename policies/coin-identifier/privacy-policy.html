Privacy Policy for Coin Identifier

Effective Date: December 27, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Coin Identifier. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information Collection

We do not collect any personal user data. The only data we process are the coin images you choose to analyze through our app.

Image Processing

When you use Coin Identifier to analyze a coin:

  • Images are sent to OpenAI's API for processing and analysis
  • We do not store or save any of the images you submit
  • Images are processed solely for the purpose of providing coin identification and information

Usage Data

We collect non-personal usage data to improve our app's functionality and user experience. This information is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date at the top of this policy.
